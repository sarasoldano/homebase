# Hi, I'm Sara 👋

Computational cognitive scientist working at the intersection of brain, behavior, and computation. MSc Cognitive Science & Neuroscience @ University of Edinburgh — moving toward industry research in neurotech and computational neuroscience.

## 🔬 Research Focus

I study how populations of neurons represent and transform information — using electrophysiology, dimensionality reduction, and behavioral experiments to link brain activity to cognition.

**Current work:** MSc dissertation analyzing large-scale Neuropixels primate electrophysiology data, using Targeted Dimensionality Reduction (TDR) and subspace alignment to investigate rotational population dynamics in working memory. Supervised by Dr. Angus Chadwick, University of Edinburgh.

**Prior work:** EEG-based perception and attention research at the Samaha Lab, UC Santa Cruz — one bioRxiv preprint, two Journal of Vision conference abstracts.
